Main Rail Suppliers

A major supplier of railway track is Hayange. The company supplies long-length, heat-treated rail. Its Sogenox(r) rail also helps to transmit train location through the track using electrical signals. This ensures that track circuits can be transmitted even in low-traffic areas, while also avoiding rail surface oxidation. The company also produces rail for major freight and passenger trains in the UK. Listed below are some other companies that make railway track.

Hayange

Corus Rail, a business unit of Corus Group plc, is the world's largest rail supplier. The company has two rail mills in the UK, Hayange and Workington, employing 514 people. In addition to manufacturing rail, Corus offers railway engineering consultancy and track maintenance services. This news follows the announcement by the French rail company Sogerail of plans to invest in Scunthorpe.

The Hayange rail plant employs 440 people and supplies railway operators around the world with up to 80-metre-long rails. Corus is Europe's second-largest steelmaker, with operations in the UK and the Netherlands. It supplies steel to automotive, construction and mechanical engineering industries. The company has also acquired a continuous casting blooming line in Volklingen. The acquisition is expected to result in significant growth for the company, and the jobs will continue to be created.

The merger between Hayange and Ascoval is the result of a three-party deal between the companies and France's national rail operator SNCF. The acquisition was needed in order to establish a national industry for green rails made from recycled steel that has been melted in electric furnaces. The move is also necessary to protect the future of French steelworks in the North, which are dependent on rails.

Tata Steel

Tata Steel, the second largest steelmaker in Europe, has secured a contract to supply steel sleepers and rail tracks to Network Rail, the company that operates Britain's railway system. The deal, which will run until 2019, will provide nearly 95 percent of the rail needed by Network Rail. Tata Steel will supply this rail to build four new railway stations in the Midlothian and Scottish Borders regions. The contract will also increase Tata Steel's production capacity at its Scunthorpe site by five per cent, making it one of the largest rail suppliers in the country. Tata Steel declined to provide financial details of the deal.

One of the benefits of Tata Steel's new rail is its extreme wear resistance. The company has developed a proprietary process to heat-treat rail in order to make it resistant to corrosion. The process involves passing the steel through a furnace that uses an electromagnetic field to heat the rail. After the steel has been heated, it is quickly cooled with compressed air. Tata Steel's Hayange facility in France and the Scunthorpe plant in the UK produce rail for this project.

Tata Steel's European operations include TATA STEEL EUROPE, the second largest steel producer in the continent. The company employs 80,000 people on four continents and produces more than half of the steel used by Indian railways. Tata Steel's French division, TATA Steel Rail France, employs 508 people and produces around 456,000 tons of rail annually. Combined with the savings from the contract, Tata Steel is a major rail supplier and the company is committed to improving its products and services to the railway industry.

Alstom

Alstom is a world leader in railway technology. Its vast product range covers all types of rail transportation and includes railway vehicles for local and regional railroads. Additionally, the company offers maintenance, repair, and modernization of all types of rolling stock, including those manufactured by other companies. Currently, Alstom supports one-third of rail travel in Great Britain. Its diverse services include a wide range of railway infrastructures and maintenance programs, including regenerative braking systems, signalling, and track maintenance.

Initially founded as a company in the 1930s, Alstom has continued to expand its market share. The company has acquired train manufacturing companies in Germany, India, and China, and is increasingly expanding into the transportation sector. Its recent acquisitions have included the train manufacturers Bombardier, Visteon, and MAN. Its products are used in more than 100 countries and have an average lifespan of around 15 years.

Air pollution is a major concern for the global human community and heavy-duty engines are the largest contributors. Alstom was an early innovator in the railway industry and has developed several freight locomotives, including the Alstom AD43C locomotive, which is capable of producing 3000 horsepower and is dedicated to heavy-duty applications in warm and dusty regions. As the world's largest rail supplier, Alstom continues to meet the requirements of clients worldwide.

Hitachi Rail System

The Hitachi Rail System is one of the world's leading railway suppliers with more than 100 years of experience in the rail industry. The company is a fully integrated railway solutions provider with facilities across Europe, Japan, the United States, and Australia. It provides rolling stock and signalling solutions for both the passenger and freight rail industry. Its services span from the design, engineering, manufacturing, and installation of infrastructure to operation and maintenance.

The company also offers integrated transportation solutions for cities and regions, delivering smarter mobility while optimizing investment. The company has a strong track record in helping cities implement smart transportation systems. Whether in a small town or large city, the company's rail solutions improve the quality of life for residents. In addition to rail systems, the company offers rail solutions that improve the efficiency and security of local transportation infrastructure. In the United States, the company is partnering with the Honolulu Authority for Rapid Transportation to construct a new, driverless metro system on Oahu.

The company has updated its SAP platform terms and conditions and their Code of Ethics. The new terms are intended to promote ethical and lawful conduct and provide the contractual means to initiate remedial action should an issue arise. In addition to local commitments, the company also aims to promote global initiatives that will cascade down to suppliers and subcontractors. These measures are designed to ensure that employees and customers benefit from an environment that is free of modern slavery.

Treadmaster

Treadmaster is a world-renowned rail flooring supplier with a long-standing reputation for reliability and safety. Its high-performance rail floor coverings meet stringent fire safety and durability standards. Treadmaster products have unrivalled industry lifecycle costs and are incredibly durable. Treadmaster also custom-engineers its flooring to fit specific rail car drawings and create bespoke designs. Its flooring is the preferred choice of major rail operators and manufacturers.

Treadmaster original products come in Diamond or Smooth patterns, which help to provide traction in any weather. The Treadmaster Diamond Pattern is the world's best anti-slip decking, combining extreme slip resistance with excellent durability and grip in wet conditions. The smooth pattern grade is textured and offers a soft and comfortable surface for sitting. It is available in several colours, including black, red, yellow, green, and white.

Using the TreadMaster Escalator is also a popular choice, since it allows cleaning of walkways and escalators with ease. Its automatic cleaning system can easily clean the entire stair tread width and also includes a handy clip-on "ESCALATOR CLEANING IN PROGRESS" sign. Once installed, TreadMaster Escalator can be easily rented or purchased. A few months of maintenance will yield years of trouble-free service for a small maintenance budget.

Tiflex

The company produces various railway products, ranging from cork and rubber bonded materials to noise attenuating materials. Their product ranges also include rail track supports and anti-slip deck coverings. Founded in 1940, Tiflex is a leader in the industry, supplying BP, Exxon Mobil and other major companies. Founded in the United Kingdom, the company has ISO 9001 and 14001 accreditations and is FPAL registered.

Treadmaster flooring is a specialised fire retardant safety rubber flooring that is the preferred choice of leading manufacturers and operators. Developed by Tiflex, Treadmaster flooring combines cork, expert technology and natural materials to provide the highest level of wear and fire retardance. This means that even if a train is knocked off course, the passengers will still be safe. They are also the safest rail flooring available, according to the UK's Government guidelines.

The company's comprehensive service offerings complement its product range. Its Treadmaster range has been in public service vehicles for decades, serving rail networks worldwide. Its Embedded Rail System is unique in that it does not need to be elevated. Moreover, the company's Technobeton subsidiary provides innovative engineering and installation services to help build railways. The company has a world-class test facility, and its rail products are manufactured in a quality management system-registered factory.
