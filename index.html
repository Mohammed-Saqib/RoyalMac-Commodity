Virtual Stockist Cards

What Is Rail Virtual Stock ? : For the first time in India or Globally , RoyalMac launched a virtual stockist ownership card, which eliminates the need for a warehouse, logistics, office, and workers to handle operations. "Hire an employee to count money as a witty statement," we simply suggested. In this segment, we may present three different cards: the Black Card, the Green Card, and the Gold Card. The black version is for novices, whereas the green version has been upgraded. The VIP grade is Gold.

1.  Black Card

Apply

The Black Card is an ownership card that will be offered in limited quantities at initially, with quantities ranging from 50 MT to 5000 MT with a profit ratio of 33% to 40% for stockists. The stockist can verify and confirm the granted quantity under the stockist's name and ownership certificate by scanning the QR code on the Black Card.The card will be valid till the shipment and transaction are completed. There is an option for the stockist to sell the stock back to RoyalMac at a mutually contract rate.

2.  Green Card

Apply

The Green Card is an upgraded ownership card with a minimum stock level of 5000 MT to 500,000 MT and a profit ratio of 33% to 40% for stockists. There will also be additional benefits for green card holders, which will be revealed at the right point. The stockist can verify and confirm the allotted quantity under the stockist's name and ownership certificate by scanning the QR code on the green card. The card will be valid till the shipment and transaction are completed. There is an option for the stockist to sell the stock back to RoyalMac at a prior offered amount.

3.  Gold Card

Apply

The Gold Card is the VIP grade card, with a minimum stock level of 500,000 MT to 1,000,000 MT and a stockist profit ratio of 33% to 40%. Gold Card holders will also receive extra privileges, such as a Dubai and European trip package. By scanning the QR code on the gold card, the commodity ownership gold card may verify and confirm the allotted quantity under the stockist's name and ownership certificate. The card will be valid till the shipping and transaction are completed. The stockist shall re-sell the stock to RoyalMac at a prior agreed-upon price.
